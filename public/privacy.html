Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. No Data Collection

The Studios-Pro CAD & 3D Viewer browser extension operates 100% locally in your web browser. We do not collect, store, log, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or file content to our servers or any third parties.

2. Permissions Usage

The extension requests the contextMenus permission. This is used solely to add the "Preview 3D/CAD in Studios-Pro" option to your right-click menu when hovering over links to STL, OBJ, GLB, and DXF files.

3. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at our main website support page.
