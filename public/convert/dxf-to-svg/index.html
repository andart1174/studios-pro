Common Questions

DXF to SVG FAQ

Everything you need to know about converting DXF to SVG.

Can I convert DXF to SVG without AutoCAD?

Yes. Studios-Pro reads DXF files natively in the browser using WebGL — no AutoCAD license or installation of any software is required.

Is the SVG output scale-accurate?

Yes. Studios-Pro preserves the original DXF coordinate system and dimensions, producing SVG files with accurate real-world measurements — critical for CNC and laser cutting applications.

What DXF versions are supported?

Studios-Pro supports the most common DXF versions including R12, R14, AutoCAD 2000, 2004, 2007, 2010, and 2013 entity types including LWPOLYLINE, ARC, CIRCLE, SPLINE, and TEXT.

Is my DXF file uploaded to any server?

No. Your DXF file is parsed and processed locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to Studios-Pro servers. Your proprietary CAD data stays completely private on your device.
