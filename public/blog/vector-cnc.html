The Problem with Raster Images Le problème avec les images matricielles (Raster)

Photos function on tiny squares of color called pixels. A CNC machine toolhead operates in coordinates (X/Y axes). Without converting those tiny squares into structural paths, your machine simply doesn't know where to move. This is where the translation process to SVG or DXF becomes crucial.
