AR 3D Viewer

Instantly project and inspect 3D models in Augmented Reality (AR) on your phone or web browser.

1 Right-click any web link to a .glb, .gltf, .stl, or .obj file.
2 Select "View in AR with Studios-Pro".
3 Scan the generated QR code with your phone camera to place the model in your physical room!
